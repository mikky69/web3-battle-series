What is Web3 Battle Series?

The Web3 Battle Series is a competitive, transparent, and entertaining format that pits two similar Web3 projects against each other in a head-to-head match. The series enables the crypto community to compare protocols, teams, technology, tokenomics, community strength, and real-world impact in an engaging way. The winner is determined exclusively by the decision of independent judges, following a professional boxing-style scoring system.
